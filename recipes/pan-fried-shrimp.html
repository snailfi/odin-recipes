Pan-Fried Shrimp

Description

A simple recipe for pan fried shrimp.

Feel free to add other seasonings you like!

Ingredients

  • 8 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 1 pound peeled and deveined bay shrimp
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice, divided
  • 1 pinch garlic salt, or to taste
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt 6 tablespoons butter in a large skillet over high heat.
  2. Add shrimp; cook and stir in hot butter until browned, about 2 minutes. Reduce heat to medium-low.
  3. Stir in 1 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice, garlic salt, salt, and pepper; cook until heated through, about 1 minute.
  4. Remove from heat; stir in remaining 2 tablespoons butter and 1 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice. Serve and enjoy!
